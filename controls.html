Disc Golf Game Instructions

How to Play

  • Click and hold mouse / touch to charge your throw.
  • Drag to aim while charging.
  • Release to throw the disc.
  • Reach the hole to complete the current hole.
  • Fewer throws = better score.

Controls

  • Mouse / Touch: Aim and throw
  • Arrow keys: Rotate camera (optional)
  • R key: Reset disc if stuck
